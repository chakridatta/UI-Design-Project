Prevention Tips

  • Avoid smoking and exposure to secondhand smoke
  • Stay active with regular exercise
  • Wear masks in polluted areas
  • Maintain good indoor air quality
  • Get vaccinated (e.g., flu and pneumonia vaccines)

How Pollution Affects Your Lungs

  • Fine particulate matter (PM2.5) can enter lungs and bloodstream
  • Causes inflammation and reduces lung function
  • Vulnerable groups: children, elderly, people with lung diseases
  • Monitor local air quality regularly
  • Limit outdoor activity during high pollution periods

Healthy Habits for Stronger Lungs

  • Practice yoga and diaphragmatic breathing
  • Exercise regularly: walking, jogging, cycling
  • Eat anti-inflammatory foods like ginger, turmeric, and berries
  • Stay hydrated and manage stress

When to See a Doctor

  • Persistent cough or wheezing
  • Shortness of breath or chest pain
  • Fatigue that doesn't improve
  • Early checkups prevent serious complications
