Brain Disorders

Click on a disorder to see its cause. Double-click for precautions.

Alzheimer's

Progressive memory loss and confusion

Parkinson's

Tremors and difficulty with movement

Epilepsy

Recurring seizures due to abnormal brain activity

Stroke

Brain damage from disrupted blood flow

Depression

Persistent sadness and lack of interest

Causes of Brain Disorders

  • Genetic mutations or family history
  • Infections such as meningitis or encephalitis
  • Traumatic brain injuries
  • Substance abuse or alcohol dependency
  • Stroke or lack of oxygen to the brain

Prevention Methods

  • Healthy diet rich in omega-3 and antioxidants
  • Regular exercise and mental stimulation
  • Avoid head injuries – wear helmets and seatbelts
  • Stress management and good sleep hygiene
  • Routine medical checkups

Common Medications

  • Donepezil and Rivastigmine for Alzheimer's
  • Levodopa for Parkinson's disease
  • Valproate and Carbamazepine for epilepsy
  • SSRIs and SNRIs for depression and anxiety
  • Anticoagulants like aspirin for stroke prevention
