Take Care of Your Heart

Heart disease is the leading cause of death globally. Educate yourself and stay healthy.

Causes

  • Unhealthy diet high in saturated fats, sugar, and salt
  • Lack of regular physical activity
  • Smoking and tobacco use
  • Excessive alcohol consumption
  • High blood pressure (hypertension)
  • High cholesterol levels
  • Diabetes or insulin resistance
  • Chronic stress and poor mental health
  • Obesity or being overweight

Prevention

  • Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains
  • Engage in at least 30 minutes of physical activity daily
  • Avoid smoking and exposure to secondhand smoke
  • Limit alcohol intake to moderate levels
  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Monitor and control blood pressure and cholesterol
  • Manage stress through relaxation techniques and hobbies
  • Get regular health check-ups
  • Sleep at least 7-8 hours a night

Common Symptoms

  • Chest pain or discomfort (angina)
  • Shortness of breath
  • Pain in arms, neck, jaw, shoulder or back
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Fatigue
  • Dizziness or lightheadedness
  • Cold sweats
  • Irregular heartbeat
