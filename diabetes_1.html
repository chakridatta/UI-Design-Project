Types of Diabetes

  • Type 1: Autoimmune condition where the pancreas produces little/no insulin.
  • Type 2: Insulin resistance or insufficient insulin production due to lifestyle.
  • Gestational: Diabetes during pregnancy, risk of future Type 2 diabetes.

Prevention Tips

  • Maintain a healthy diet (low sugar/carbs).
  • Exercise regularly (30 mins daily).
  • Monitor blood sugar levels.
  • Maintain a healthy weight.
